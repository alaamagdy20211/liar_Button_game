How To Play

  • Use ⬆ ⬇ ⬅ ➡ arrow keys to move the snake.
  • Eat the food to increase your score and grow longer.
  • To WIN a level, you must reach the required score.
  • Once you reach the score, you advance to the next level.
  • Each new level is faster and more challenging.
  • Avoid hitting the walls, holes, or your own body.
